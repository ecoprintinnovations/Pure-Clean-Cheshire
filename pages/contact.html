Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Can't find what you need? Email us or call.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3–5 days ahead for the best availability. End-of-tenancy and end-of-month dates fill quickly, so book as soon as you know your move-out date. Same-day and next-day bookings are sometimes possible (+25% urgency supplement) — call us to check.

Do I need to be at the property during the clean?

No. Most clients leave keys with us, their letting agent, or in a lockbox. We'll confirm key collection arrangements when booking. Our team is fully insured, DBS-checked, and we provide photo proof after every clean so you can verify the work remotely.

What's included in the price?

Every quote includes:

  • All cleaning materials and professional equipment
  • Eco-friendly, pet-safe cleaning products
  • Fully insured (£5M public liability), DBS-checked team
  • Photo proof sent after completion
  • Signed checklist for your records
  • 7-day satisfaction guarantee (free re-clean if needed)
  • No hidden fees, no hourly charges
Is carpet cleaning included in end of tenancy cleans?

Standard vacuuming is included. Professional hot-water extraction carpet cleaning is an add-on (from £35/room, 20% off when bundled). Most letting agents and inventory clerks require professional carpet cleaning for deposit return — we strongly recommend adding it.

Do you provide cleaning for Airbnb/holiday lets?

Yes — we specialise in same-day turnovers for short-term lets. Services include: guest-ready clean (2–4 hours), linen/towel change, hotel-standard bed making, welcome pack setup, photo report after every clean, key holding, and flexible scheduling for back-to-back bookings. From £70/turnover.

What if I'm not happy with the clean?

Our 7-day satisfaction guarantee means we'll return to re-clean any missed areas at no extra cost. Just let us know within 7 days with photos if possible. We prioritise corrective re-cleans over refunds — our goal is to get it right.

How do I pay?

Pay after the clean, only when you're satisfied. We accept cash, card (contactless/chip & PIN), bank transfer, and mobile payments. Invoice provided on the day for your records. No upfront deposits required.

Are your products safe for pets and children?

Yes. All our cleaning solutions are non-toxic, biodegradable, phosphate-free, and safe for children, pets, and sensitive skin. We can also use fragrance-free products on request — just let us know when booking.

Do you cover my postcode?

We cover a 15-mile radius from Frodsham (WA6, CH2, WA7, WA4, WA5 and surrounding villages). Enter your postcode in the quote form and we'll confirm coverage and exact pricing within 2 hours. Areas outside our radius may incur a small travel supplement.

Can you work with my letting agent?

Absolutely. We regularly liaise with letting agents for key collection, access, and handover. We provide the signed checklist and photo proof they need for deposit processing. Just share their details when booking and we'll handle the coordination.
