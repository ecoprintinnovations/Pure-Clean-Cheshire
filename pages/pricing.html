What Affects Your Final Price

We believe in transparent pricing. These are the factors that can adjust your quote up or down.

Property Condition

Well-maintained properties sit at the starting price. Heavy limescale, baked-on grease, mould, or pet hair adds time and specialist products.

Number of Bathrooms

An en-suite or second bathroom adds cleaning time. Properties with 3+ bathrooms typically require an extended team.

Furnished vs Unfurnished

Empty properties are faster to clean. Furniture in place means working around items and accessing hidden areas.

Location & Access

Our prices cover Frodsham & surrounding areas. Limited parking, upper floors without lift, or difficult access may add a small supplement.

Timing & Urgency

Standard booking: 3+ days notice. Same-day/next-day: +25%. Weekends & bank holidays: +15%. End-of-month peak: book early.

Additional Services

Carpet cleaning, oven deep clean, internal windows, sanitisation, garden tidy — all available as discounted add-ons.
