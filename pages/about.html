About Pure Clean Cheshire

A family-run business built on trust, quality, and a genuine care for the homes and properties we clean across Cheshire.

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Pure Clean Cheshire began with one van, two founders, and a simple promise: deliver a cleaner that’s reliable, transparent, and genuinely careful with every property. Today we operate across Frodsham, Helsby, Elton, Ince, Runcorn, Warrington, and the wider Cheshire area, supporting landlords, short-let hosts, and homeowners with a consistent, local team.

This isn’t a franchise model or a gig platform. We’re a small owner-led team that still answers calls, reviews properties personally, and trains every cleaner to our own standard. Most of our new business comes from existing clients and agent referrals, which keeps us focused on quality rather than quantity.

Every team member is DBS-checked, insured under our policy, and required to follow sequenced checklists designed for inventory, guest-ready, and deep-clean standards. We treat every visit as if we’re preparing the property for our own family.
